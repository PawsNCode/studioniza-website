About images: what 'included' actually means.

When you pick a tier with images included, here's what that covers: image SEO on all your existing photos (alt text, file naming, compression, WebP conversion), background removal and basic retouching for consistency, and up to 2 lifestyle or infographic images per product, generated using AI tools where suitable.

What it doesn't cover: original product photography, multi-angle reconstruction from a single bad photo, 3D renders, or model photography. AI generation has limits, especially for products like jewelry, custom items, and intricate textures, and I won't fabricate angles I can't actually replicate. If a product can't be reasonably enhanced from your reference photos, the SEO work for that product still happens. Just without the additional images.

The full image policy is in your contract.

About FAQ schema: what changed in May 2026.

On May 7, 2026, Google retired FAQ rich snippets. The expandable Q&A dropdowns that used to appear under organic search results are gone. Most SEO services still list "FAQ schema for rich snippets" as a deliverable. I won't, because it's no longer accurate.

Here's what's still true: FAQ schema remains valid Schema.org markup, and Google explicitly stated they'll keep using it to understand pages. More importantly, AI search engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini parse FAQ schema heavily when deciding which pages to cite. The schema isn't dead. Its job changed.

So I still add FAQ schema where it genuinely helps: top product and collection pages, policy pages, and anywhere clear question-and-answer content exists. The framing on this page reflects the new reality, not the old one. If a competitor's proposal still promises "FAQ rich snippets for higher CTR," that's a sign their template hasn't been updated.

FAQ

Common questions about Shopify SEO.

How long until I see results?

Honestly: 4 to 12 weeks for early ranking improvements, 3 to 6 months for meaningful traffic increases. Anyone who promises faster is either lying or running paid ads they're calling SEO. Google indexes your changes within days, but actually moving up in rankings depends on competition for your keywords, your domain age, and how often you publish new content. I'll send you the indexing confirmations so you can track progress yourself.

Do I keep this work if I cancel?

Yes. SEO is permanent infrastructure work. Optimized pages stay optimized. I don't host anything for you. I just make changes inside your Shopify admin. Cancel anytime. Your store keeps everything I built.

Why is your SEO more expensive than competitors I see on Fiverr?

Most cheap SEO services are template work. They fill in the same generic meta descriptions across hundreds of products without keyword research, schema, or strategy. My pricing reflects custom keyword research per page, real Product and Organization schema, image SEO, internal linking, GEO optimization for AI search engines, and indexing follow-up. If your goal is to actually rank in Google and get cited in ChatGPT, this is the floor of what real SEO costs. If you want template work, I'm not the right fit.

Can I do a smaller test project first?

Absolutely. That's exactly what the Starter tier ($499) is for. 25 pages, full scope, no images. Most clients who start there move up to Growth or Scale within 60 days because they see the rankings improvement.

What if I run a small store with only 10 products?

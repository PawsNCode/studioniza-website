How payments work.

One-time projects (SEO and Blog content): 50% upfront to begin work. 50% on delivery before access transfers. Payment via PayPal Business, Wise, or bank transfer.

Recurring services (Chatbots and Reviews): 100% of setup fee upfront, plus first month's recurring with the same invoice. Auto-pay required after that.

Minimum commitments on recurring: Chatbots: 90 days for Basic and Advanced, 60 days for Premium. Reviews: 60 days across all tiers. After the minimum, cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

Refunds: 14-day money-back guarantee on the first deliverable of any one-time project. Once approved, the project is locked in. Recurring services are pro-rated only if cancellation is my fault. Full refund policy is in your contract.
